Getting Started

How do I create my first trading record?

To create your first trading record:

  1. Open TradeFlex and tap the "+" button
  2. Select "Add Trade"
  3. Choose the asset type (Stock, Crypto, Forex, etc.)
  4. Enter the trade details (symbol, quantity, price, date)
  5. Select whether it's a Buy or Sell transaction
  6. Tap "Save" to record the trade
Tip: You can also import trades from CSV files if you have existing records.
What information do I need to track my trades?

For accurate tracking, you should record:

Required Information:
  • Asset symbol (e.g., AAPL, BTC)
  • Trade type (Buy/Sell)
  • Quantity/Volume
  • Price per unit
  • Trade date and time
Optional Information:
  • Trading fees/commissions
  • Notes or strategy used
  • Portfolio/account name
  • Tags for categorization

Trading Records Management

How do I import trades from my broker?

You can import trades using CSV files:

  1. Export your trade history from your broker as a CSV file
  2. In TradeFlex, go to Settings → Import/Export
  3. Tap "Import CSV"
  4. Select your CSV file and map the columns to TradeFlex fields
  5. Review the preview and confirm the import
Note: Make sure your CSV file includes all required fields (symbol, type, quantity, price, date).
Can I edit or delete trades after adding them?

Yes, you can modify your trades at any time:

To Edit a Trade:
  1. Find the trade in your trade list
  2. Tap on the trade to open details
  3. Tap the "Edit" button
  4. Make your changes and save
To Delete a Trade:
  1. Open the trade details
  2. Tap the "Delete" button
  3. Confirm the deletion
Note: Deleted trades cannot be recovered unless you have a backup.

Analytics & Reports

How are profit and loss calculated?

TradeFlex calculates P&L using the FIFO (First In, First Out) method:

Example:
  • Buy: 100 shares of AAPL at $150 = $15,000
  • Sell: 50 shares of AAPL at $160 = $8,000
  • Profit: (160 - 150) × 50 = $500

The app automatically accounts for:

  • Trading fees and commissions
  • Currency conversions
  • Stock splits and dividends
  • Wash sale rules (where applicable)
Can I export reports for tax purposes?

Yes, TradeFlex provides several export options for tax reporting:

Available Formats:
  • PDF reports
  • Excel spreadsheets
  • CSV files
  • Tax-specific templates
Report Types:
  • Realized gains/losses
  • Unrealized positions
  • Dividend income
  • Trading summary
Important: Always consult with a tax professional for specific tax advice.

Troubleshooting

The app is running slowly or crashing

Try these troubleshooting steps:

Quick Fixes:
  1. Force close and restart the app
  2. Restart your device
  3. Check for app updates
  4. Free up device storage space
Advanced Solutions:
  1. Clear app cache (Android)
  2. Reinstall the app
  3. Check device compatibility
  4. Contact support with crash logs
Before reinstalling: Make sure to backup your data to avoid losing your trading records.
How do I backup and restore my data?

Protect your trading data with regular backups:

To Create a Backup:
  1. Go to Settings → Backup & Restore
  2. Tap "Create Backup"
  3. Choose backup location (local/cloud)
  4. Wait for backup completion
To Restore Data:
  1. Go to Settings → Backup & Restore
  2. Tap "Restore from Backup"
  3. Select your backup file
  4. Confirm the restore operation
Recommendation: Set up automatic daily backups to ensure your data is always protected.
