v1.0.0
November 4, 2025
Initial Release

TradeFlex Launch

The first public release of TradeFlex, bringing professional trading record management to both mobile devices and desktop.

Core Features
  • Multiple portfolio management
  • Record long and short trades
  • Record exchange trades
  • Record dividends
  • Position information with flexible sub-position system
  • Detailed profit/loss calculations
  • Rich statistics and charts
  • Data backup and restore
  • Pure local data storage
  • Strategy tags
  • Deposit and withdrawal management
  • Custom asset definitions
